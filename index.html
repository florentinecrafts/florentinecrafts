About Us

Design & Manufacturing company

Florentine Crafts has a very strong product backed-up by our strong product designers, skilled craftsmen, to suit varied preferences and budgets range, producing only the best quality products with professional management skills. We believe in creating the best products for the best companies out there. Florentine serves reputed international fashion retailers, department stores and brands as original design manufacturer (ODM).The company has expertise and production facilities in the field of HIGH QUALITY LEATHER HANDBAGS, LEATHER BELTS, SMALL LEATHER GOODS, TRAVEL BAGS AND LEATHER ACCESSORIES.
We are a socially responsible and environmentally compliant organisation headquartered in Mumbai - India, having leather goods manufacturing facilities at Kanpur, Kolkata, and Chennai.

REUSE RECYCLE REBORN

Florentine Crafts is passionate about sustainable product designs. We are invested in sourcing that create new products from materials that can be reused, recycled and reborn into a new product. We can help your company reach your sustainability goals and work together to protect our living planet, Earth.
